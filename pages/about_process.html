The WebCraft Process

My proven 5-phase methodology ensures every project delivers exceptional results. Here's exactly what you can expect when we work together.

01

Discovery & Strategy

Week 1 - Foundation Setting

We start with deep-dive sessions to understand your business, goals, and target audience. I analyze your competition, define success metrics, and create a strategic roadmap that aligns with your business objectives.

What You Get:

  • Comprehensive business analysis report
  • Competitive landscape overview
  • Strategic project roadmap
  • Success metrics definition
02

Design & Planning

Week 2-3 - Visual Foundation

I create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes that bring your vision to life. Every design decision is backed by user experience research and conversion optimization principles.

What You Get:

  • Custom wireframes and mockups
  • Interactive prototypes
  • Brand style guide
  • User experience optimization plan
03

Development & Testing

Week 3-5 - Building Excellence

Using cutting-edge technologies and best practices, I build your website with performance, security, and scalability in mind. Rigorous testing ensures everything works perfectly across all devices and browsers.

What You Get:

  • Clean, optimized code
  • Cross-browser compatibility
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • Performance optimization
04

Launch & Optimization

Week 6 - Going Live

I handle the technical launch process and monitor performance closely. Post-launch optimization ensures your site performs at its best from day one, with analytics setup and initial SEO implementation.

What You Get:

  • Seamless deployment process
  • Analytics and tracking setup
  • Initial SEO optimization
  • Performance monitoring
05

Ongoing Partnership

Continuous Growth

Your website is a living asset that grows with your business. I provide ongoing support, maintenance, and strategic improvements to ensure your digital presence continues delivering results.

What You Get:

  • Regular maintenance and updates
  • Performance monitoring
  • Strategic growth recommendations
